Privacy Policy for Minimal Slot

We are not interested in collecting personal data. Minimal Slot exist simply as a game to be played.

We do not store or transmit your personal information and do not use advertising or analytics software.

What Information is collected?

Minimal Slot does not collect personal information and does not connect to the internet. Any information generated remains on the device.

Email

If you email the developer for support or other feedback, the emails and email addresses will be retained for quality assurance purposes.

The emails and email addresses will be used solely to respond to concerns or suggestions given and never for marketing purposes.
